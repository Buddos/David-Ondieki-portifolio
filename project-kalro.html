KALRO Data Management

Industrial Attachment Case Study · 2024

Laboratory Data Management at KALRO

2024
KALRO (Kenya Agricultural & Livestock Research Organization)
Supervisor: Victor Muhoma

Project Overview

During my industrial attachment at KALRO, I gained hands-on experience in laboratory data management and sample administration. The role involved managing sample reception and archiving, performing accurate data entry into laboratory information systems, and maintaining meticulous documentation in compliance with safety and confidentiality protocols.

Sample Reception

Received and logged samples with complete chain-of-custody documentation

Sample Archiving

Organized and maintained proper storage of biological and chemical samples

Data Entry

Accurate and efficient data entry into laboratory management systems (98% accuracy)

Compliance

Maintained security protocols and confidentiality standards throughout

Key Responsibilities

Sample Administration

Managed the complete lifecycle of laboratory samples from reception through archiving, ensuring proper labeling, storage conditions, and documentation for traceability and compliance purposes.

Specific Duties:

  • Received incoming samples and verified completeness of documentation
  • Performed data entry into the Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS)
  • Organized samples in appropriate storage facilities with proper environmental controls
  • Maintained detailed logs and tracking systems for sample location and status
  • Ensured compliance with biosafety and confidentiality protocols
  • Generated reports and provided data support to laboratory teams

Performance Metrics

Accuracy Level: 98%

Maintained a 98% accuracy rate in data entry and sample administration, demonstrating meticulous attention to detail and strong organizational skills. This high accuracy level was critical for maintaining data integrity in a research organization where precision is paramount.

Achievements:

  • Zero protocol violations during the attachment period
  • Improved sample tracking efficiency through systematic organization
  • Consistently met data entry deadlines with high accuracy
  • Received positive feedback from laboratory supervisors
  • Demonstrated reliability in handling sensitive research materials

About KALRO

The Kenya Agricultural & Livestock Research Organization (KALRO) is a premier research organization dedicated to advancing agricultural and livestock science in Kenya. During my attachment, I worked within KALRO's laboratory divisions, gaining exposure to professional research practices and standards used in East Africa's leading agricultural research institution.

Professional Development

This industrial attachment provided invaluable insights into professional laboratory operations and the critical importance of data management in research. The experience demonstrated how accurate, organized data systems support the entire research process and contribute to organizational success. This foundation has equipped me well for future roles in biomedical research environments where data integrity is paramount.
